Jacob Zaionz

Jacob Zaionz is a member of this team and aspires to go into UX/UI Design. In his free time, he enjoys cooking, trying new recipes, playing musical instruments, and spending time with his family and friends.

During the first team meeting, Jacob mentioned to his teammates that he had a hard time paying attention to class because he was busy cooking dinner, which quickly led to the idea of creating Flavor Finder! He helped create a storyboard template before creating the initial wireframes, and created the website that you're seeing today!
